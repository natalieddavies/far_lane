What a meaningful research question for the next stage would be

I would go one of two ways.

The first avenue would be to look deeper into women in Industry and attempt an indepth analysis of the pay rates in various roles within the industry to find out if there is still a gender pay gap. A research statement for this could be "A gender pay gap still exists within the mining industry".

The second avenue I would consider going down would be to weigh up the effect of FIFO works travelling to minesites via plane and the greenhouse gases involved in these journeys. This would be interesting as I could compare if the push for greener commodities is weighed out by increased air travel as more work arises. A research statement for this could be "The greenhouse gas emitions as a result of the air travel of FIFO workers cancels out the mining industries achievement in cleaner commodities"

A dot point methodology that outlines your approach
